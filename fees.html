How to register ?

Call us or send us an email to have an interview with one of our instructor in order to recommand you the best course.

After the interview please fill the form with your personal information and choose the best schedule according for your availability

The last step you have to pay for 6 months in advance and you will get personal Big gym card. This card allow you to access to our gym any time and to be benificary of our discounts and offers.
